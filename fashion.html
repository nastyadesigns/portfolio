Music's Fashion is an exhibition that is dedicated to
young people's culture, particularly music and fashion.
Exhibition tells us the answer on the following quetsion:
How the music influences the style of the young ones?
With the help of an app the exhibition becomes interactive.
In the app the user can listen to the interview of the young ones, from Milan and Rotterdam who are shown on the artwork.
User has a possibility to share their own story in the app.

Following products were made:

  • Logo
  • Letterhead paper
  • Buisness Cards
  • Posters (and artworks)
  • Flyer
  • Introduction board
  • Web-app



Tools

Adobe Illustrator
Adobe Photoshop
Adobe InDesign
HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Bootstrap 4)

Goal:

The goal is to create corporate identity and app for
interactive exhibition, that is dedicated to young people culture.
This exhibition tells us about music and fashion.

App is made for interaction.


In the team with

Isabel Rinkel


Work process

As starting point, we made a research: interview with
the young ones form Rotterdam and Milan.
During the interview, we made photo portraits of the participants
After reviewing the results we came up with the idea of exhibition.
Next, the artworks were created, that were used for the posters.
We worked with bright colors to create energetic and warm atmosphere.
On the artworks were used elements from the music albums,
that the interviewed young ones are listening to
and of course mentioned the photo portraits.

Then, we designed the logo. It had to be modern
and simple, to give the message clearly.

Then we made sketches and design proposals
of the cards and letterhead paper.

Also, there were made wireframes, sketches
and design proposals of the web-app. In the app
artworks were also used.

The idea is, when the user
approaches the artwork on the expo,
with the help of the app, they can listen to the
stories of the person who is in the artwork.
In the end there is possibility to share own story and
send pictures.
As result, there is possibilty that there will
be the artwork created using user's pictures.
