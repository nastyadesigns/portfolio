Cusfo is a small buisness run by 2 young graphic designers.
The company specializes on logos design, corporate identity
and media-expressions.
The following products were made:

  • Logo and slogan
  • Letter paper
  • Buisness cards
  • Instagram visual
  • Website

Tools

Adobe Illustrator
Adobe Photoshop
Adobe InDesign
HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Bootstrap 4)

Goal:

The goal is to make unique corporate identity
that combines two styles of the designers.


In the team with

Isabel Rinkel


Work process

First, we created a styleboard. Main features were:
Customer oriented, Feminine and Social.
We used colors like gentle red and rose.
Our goal was to capture feminine and light style
but also show that Cusfo is a buisness.

Then, we made some idea-sketches of the logo. Cusfo is
a letter game of the word “Focus” and we
indicated it.

Then we made sketches and design proposals
of the cards and letter paper.

Also we made wireframes, sketches and design proposals
of the website.
