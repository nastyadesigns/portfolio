As a personal project I regulary create Book Covers
for the author Dana Tales. I work in the collage technique
and create covers in postmodern and minimalistic style










Tools:

Adobe Photoshop


Goal:

The goal is to create a book cover that represents
part of the story or the atmosphere of the book.
Also all the book covers of the novels from this author
have to have similair style and be unique
at the same time.


Work process

First, I make a research to get to know
the atmosphere of the stroy and characters.

Then, I experiment with composition,
fonts and images, creating different versions
Then, the author picks one of the design proposals.


I am using textures, but restrict to not more than 3 colors.
I download stock images and with use of collage technique
create something of my own.
