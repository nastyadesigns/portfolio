Hangtag for the new collection
of denim clothes of the brand Blue Barn Jeans.






Tools:

Adobe Illustrator


Goal:

The goal is to show that the product is organic
and it's made for the children and preteens.


Work process

First, I created a moodboard that helped to discuss
look and feel of the hangtag with the client.

Then I made idea-sketches using colors like green
and pastel blue and pink.

Next, I made a couple of variations of the design.
Through the process, I showed what I made to the client.
As the result, client chose the design he prefered, then
I worked it out and sent to the typography.
